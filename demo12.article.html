History of Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born on February 19, 1630, in Shivneri Fort. He was known for his bravery, leadership, and smart war tactics. He used guerrilla warfare and built a strong navy to protect his kingdom. Shivaji Maharaj focused on good rule, respect for all religions, and the well-being of his people.
